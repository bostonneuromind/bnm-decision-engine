1. About This Form

This form requests parental/legal guardian consent for your child to participate in the Decision Catcher (CNA v7) beta program by Boston Neuromind LLC.

2. Child's Assent

Your child has already provided their assent. Parental consent is required for participation as a minor under 18.

3. Study Procedures

  • Total 8 weeks (4-8 week protocol + 4-week outcome)
  • Initial assessment, weekly progress entries
  • Final outcome assessment

4. Data Protection (HIPAA + COPPA)

All your child's information is protected per HIPAA standards. For children under 13, COPPA additionally applies. Data is not shared with third parties.

5. Right to Withdraw

You may withdraw your child from this study at any time without penalty. All data will be deleted within 30 days.

6. Safety Protocol

Under Mandated Reporter duty, the following will be reported immediately:

  • Imminent risk of harm to your child
  • Suicidal ideation/attempt
  • Suspected abuse
