1. Study Overview

This beta program is a non-commercial beta test to evaluate the clinical utility of the Decision Catcher (CNA v7) system developed by Boston Neuromind LLC. The system supports clinician decision-making through differential diagnosis, comorbidity decomposition, and adaptive training recommendations.

2. Participation Duration & Procedure

  • Total 8 weeks (4-8 week protocol + 4 week outcome measurement)
  • Initial assessment (60-90 min)
  • Weekly progress entry (5-10 min/session)
  • Final outcome assessment (60 min)

3. Potential Benefits

You may receive an objective assessment of your cognitive/emotional state and personalized training recommendations. However, this system does not replace your clinician's diagnosis, and system recommendations serve only as a support tool for the clinician's final decision.

4. Potential Risks

This beta system is a clinical decision support tool, with the following potential risks:

  • Possible inaccuracy of system recommendations (beta stage)
  • Possible psychological discomfort during assessment
  • Possible technical errors in data processing

To minimize these risks, all decisions are reviewed by your clinician, and crisis situations (suicidal crisis, psychosis, etc.) are immediately reported to the clinician.

5. Confidentiality & Data Protection

All your identifying information and clinical data are encrypted and stored according to HIPAA standards. They are not disclosed to third parties without your consent, and for research disclosure, only anonymized data with all identifying information removed is used.

6. Right to Withdraw

You may withdraw from this beta program at any time without penalty. Upon withdrawal, all your data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. Safety Protocol

The following situations are immediately reported to the clinician (Mandated Reporter obligation):

  • Concern of imminent harm to self or others
  • Active suicidal ideation or attempt
  • Suspected abuse of children, elderly, or disabled persons
