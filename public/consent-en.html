1. Study Overview

This beta program is a non-commercial pilot to evaluate the clinical utility of Decision Catcher (CNA v7), developed by Boston Neuromind LLC. The system supports clinical decision-making through differential diagnosis, comorbidity decomposition, and adaptive training recommendations.

2. Duration & Procedures

  • Total 8 weeks (4-8 week protocol + 4-week outcome assessment)
  • Initial assessment (60-90 minutes), one time
  • Weekly progress entry (5-10 minutes each)
  • Final outcome assessment (60 minutes)

3. Potential Benefits

You may receive objective assessment of cognitive/emotional status and personalized training recommendations. However, this system does not replace a clinician's diagnosis — recommendations serve only as supporting information for clinical decisions.

4. Potential Risks

This beta system is a clinical decision support tool, with the following potential risks:

  • Possible inaccuracy in system recommendations (beta stage)
  • Possible psychological discomfort during assessment
  • Possible technical errors in data processing

To minimize these risks, all decisions are finalized by clinicians. Crisis situations (suicidal ideation, psychosis, etc.) are immediately reported to a clinician.

5. Confidentiality & Data Protection

All identifying and clinical information is encrypted per HIPAA standards. Information will not be disclosed to third parties without consent. For research disclosure, only de-identified anonymous data is used.

6. Right to Withdraw

You may withdraw from this beta program at any time without penalty. Upon withdrawal, all your data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. Safety Protocol

The following situations will be reported to a clinician immediately (Mandated Reporter duty):

  • Imminent risk of harm to self or others
  • Active suicidal ideation or suicide attempt
  • Suspected abuse of children, elderly, or disabled persons
